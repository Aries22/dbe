ABOUT US

Welcome to De-Bridge Elite Business Consulting Limited
We are an indigenous management and business advisory firm, founded in 2004, catering to clients across Africa, Asia, Europe and UAE. We have our local offices in Abuja, Port-Harcourt, Lagos, Kano & Kaduna. We are headquartered in the UK with other international offices in India and Dubai. Our people include industry-specific specialists and subject matter experts from various fields ranging from information technology, knowledge management, pharmacology, legal, architecture and real estate etc.
At DBE, excellence with integrity is what we strive for and achieve consistently. We display our exceptional business acumen in providing you high-quality tailor-made solutions. We consistently empower our clientele with technology, software, advisory, HR services, and outsourcing solutions. We are passionate about our clients’ growth and success which is a yardstick for our success.
We never lose sight that people, systems, strategy and structure are crucial to the success of our clients. Therefore, we are committed to creating solutions, developing methodologies, nurturing talent, implementing processes and fine-tuning organizational set-up. We help our clients discover exceptional leaders, winning teams & successful organizations. Our scope of assignments and projects include:
