HUMAN CAPITAL MANAGEMENT

Our collection of management training programmes are geared to provide individuals with a host of tools and techniques that will better-prepare them for the continuous and diverse challenges that come with the responsibilities of this role. Our management courses focus on managing self, managing others, management as a set of tools and management as a skill.

Managing the HR Function
This intensive, hands-on three -day programme comprehensive is designed to give participants a solid grasp of all functional areas of the Human Resources Management providing operational information and guidance for individuals newly charged with HR responsibilities or wishing to expand their HR current skill set. Delegates will learn the essentials of key human resources management functions and how they tie in to corporate performance.  

HR Policy Development and Auditing
This course is designed to empower HR practitioners draft organizational/ human resource policies that are feasible and implementable. Delegates would be equipped with skills to write policies can be easily understood, interpreted and implemented, thereby making monitoring and measuring easy. The program is for those who draft policies to be able to decide on a framework for policies, producing consistency and quality in terms of luggage and format.  

Performance Management: A Practical Approach
This course helps to create an understanding of the performance management process and how to use performance management to develop a superior workforce. It will guide participants’ an understanding of performance management that goes beyond performance appraisals or annual reviews. Delegates would learn to set performance goals based on overall corporate strategy as well as interpret data and understand deviations.  

Career Planning and Talent Management
The course is designed to enhance participants’ skills and knowledge in career planning and talent management as critical tools for bringing out the best in talents within the organization. Participants will learn how to help employees acquire the competencies that meet strategic challenges, promote individual growth and organizational productivity as well as create a corporation that is aligned to its employees career progression.  Learn how to walk your employee through Career Development Plans and be part of their journey from the moment they join you!

Corporate Strategy
Our collection of management training programmes are geared to provide individuals with a host of tools and techniques that will better-prepare them for the continuous and diverse challenges that come with the responsibilities of this role. Our management courses focus on managing self, managing others, management as a set of tools and management as a skill.

Business Process Mapping and Improvement
This course is designed to enable you document, understand and improve your organization’s business processes. It will help you to gain a better understanding of what process mapping entails and ultimately, offers the disciplines and methodologies needed to transform organization into agile enterprises. Delegates will leave the course with the knowledge and tools for documenting, mapping and analyzing business processes in order to improve overall organizational efficiency.  

Creating a Blue Ocean Strategy
This executive program is designed for decision makers in organizations looking to achieve stronger profitable growth as well as innovatively adapt along the lines of customer needs. It will offer insight into organizational strategy formulation and execution, as well as how the Blue Ocean Strategy concept is a step ahead, and still leans on traditional frameworks for creating strategy. 

Strategic Management
This programme has been designed to provide the participants with an understanding of the nature and dynamics of strategic management in achieving organization’s goals and objectives. It will help the participants learn and apply useful analytical framework, tools and models used at different stages of developing a successful competitive advantage.   

Building and Sustaining Strategy for Competitive Advantage
In today’s highly turbulent macroeconomic environment, the threat of competition and the quest to overcome it is a constant challenge for businesses. This programme equips participants with the knowledge required to overcome the threat of fierce competition, which is a constant strain on many businesses today. Building and Sustaining Strategy for Competitive Advantage is a two-day programme which introduces and examines new and innovative approaches to break the traditional rules of strategy – rules that restrain most businesses – in order to identify new sources of value.
