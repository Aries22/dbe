PERSONAL DEVELOPMENT

The following training is available:

Effective Business Writing Skills
The Effective Business Writing Skills for Beginners training will introduce participants to the concept of business writing and show them how to communicate concisely, clearly and effectively.  This program is designed to show participants how to plan, organise and structure their writing to achieve better results with written business communication. 

High Impact Business Presentation: Turning numerical data in graphical charts
The High Impact Business Communication Programme is a three part programme comprising Effective Business Writing, Effective Presentation Skills and Turning graphs and turning numerical data into graphical charts. This programme is designed to equip the participants with the competencies required to communicate effectively in writing, presentation of data and speaking.  

Personal Effectiveness & Mastery
The Personal Mastery course is designed to equip participants to become more effective managers of themselves interact better with others and manage their own workloads. It will offer skills and knowledge that help an employee boost personal performance and productivity. The training is a cocktail of cutting edge principles for creating successful outcomes, increasing motivation and becoming a better individual. The class is taught from the stand point of how personal mastery impacts business performance in the workplace.  

Effective Administration for P. A’s and Secretaries
The Effective Administration course is a high impact program for personal assistants and secretaries to enhance their capacity for multitasking, back to back administration and managing support services that ensure organizational efficiency. This course which focuses on key organization, communication, and time management skills aims to help participants create and manage the right working office environment whilst ensuring that organizational goals are met.
